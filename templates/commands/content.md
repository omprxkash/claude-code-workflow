description: Turn one source into multiple content pieces
---

Take the source I provide ($ARGUMENTS, a pasted text, or a linked/attached file) and
repurpose it into a content pack. Keep the core message and facts intact across all
of them.

Produce:

1. **Summary** — 3–5 sentences capturing the main idea.
2. **Key points** — 5–8 bullet takeaways.
3. **Short posts** — 3 standalone social posts, each a different angle, each able to
   stand on its own.
4. **Thread** — a 5–8 part thread that builds an argument or walks through the idea.
5. **Headline options** — 5 headlines/subject lines, varied in style.

Tone: match the source (or what I specify). No hype, no clickbait that the content
can't back up. If the source is thin or unclear, tell me what's missing before
generating.
